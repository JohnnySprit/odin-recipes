Pho

Description

Pho is a very tasty Vietnamese noodle dish that is very often made with beef and topped with bean sprouts, basil, and asian chilies.

Ingredients You'll Need:

  • Ginger
  • Onions
  • Beef Marrow
  • Beef Chuck
  • Water
  • Scallions
  • Fish Sauce
  • Star Anise
  • Cloves
  • Cinnamon Stick
  • Fennel Seeds
  • Coriander Seeds
  • Salt
  • Pho Noodles
  • Beef Sirloin

Optional Garnishes

  • Sliced Chilies
  • Thinly Sliced Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Bean Sprouts
  • Thai Basil
  • Lime
  • Sauces (Hoisin and/or Sriracha)

Key Steps

  1. Pre-Boil the beef bones and meat in a large metal cooking vat for 3-4 hours. Remember to scoop out the scum continously to clean.
  2. Char the ginger, onions, and other spices in the oven until browned
  3. Add Fish Sauce to taste, and assemble the dish!
